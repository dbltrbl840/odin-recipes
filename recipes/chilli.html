Chilli con carne

Description

This spicy Mexican beef and bean stew is a real crowd-pleaser that the whole family will love.

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 500 gram lean minced beef
  • 2 medium sized onions finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 3 long green chillis, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 teapsoon ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 can of red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup of beef stock
  • 1/4 cup of coriander leaves
  • steamed rice for serving
  • sour cream for serving
